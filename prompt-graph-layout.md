Adds a new lib/graph-layout.ts module implementing a force-directed simulation (D3-force style, but written from scratch in pure TS to avoid adding dependencies and to keep tight control over IFS-specific constraints). Wires it into both PartsMapCanvas (with persisted positions, pin-on-drag, and a "Reset Layout" button) and MeetingRelMap (one-shot layout, no persistence, runs once when the map first appears). Adds Focus Mode to both (tap a part → all unrelated edges/labels dim heavily). Includes a small label-collision-avoidance pass for feeling labels. Includes a diagnostic step for the tap issue, with a hit-radius bump.
